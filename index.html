about us

“Kings Raid “ was established in 2015. We are small group of Yezdi and Jawa motorcycle riders from Karnataka. We tour on Jawa Yezdi bikes on regular basis , it’s a great time for bikers and families to hang out and enjoy the company for friendly bikers , some great scenic places and of course the Yezdi & Jawa bikes. The sole dream of our members is to run these beautiful machines as long as we can , and to cherish every moment while doing that and pass on the legacy. The intention of this website is just to share some good photographs, videos and stories of our rides. ..... the twin beats lives on!!!
